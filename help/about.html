About JKM Edit

Empowering creators and professionals with privacy-first, lightning-fast digital utility tools.

Our Mission

We started JKM Edit to make everyday file and data work easier. The goal is to provide useful, free tools that are simple to use and keep your files on your device whenever possible.

How it works

Most online tools upload data to remote servers. That can be slower and less private. JKM Edit is built so the work stays in your browser when possible, and we do not keep copies of your files.

Who uses it

Students, freelancers, and small businesses use these tools for quick edits, file conversions, and simple calculations. The site is designed to work smoothly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
