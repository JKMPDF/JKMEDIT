Our Story

JKM Edit was formed in 2025 having a simple idea of creating a space where one can find the good online tools, learn new digital tricks, vivo everyday lives with no heavy software or subscriptions.

JKM Edit was formed by Mahendra Purohit, who started making websites, edit utilities and tools with good fondness of digital making and technology. What started as a hobby project, became a big web for many people to use handy online solutions.

The JKM Edit website was first time a simple utility page with some content experiments. Later on, the website has evolved into various category like PDF tools, Image tools, Finance utilities, File Converters, Productivity, Blogs and Learning. Each time with a single thought, different and making a real value for people.

The audience of the blog started growing and the idea behind the JKM Edit also changed. But JKM Edit has not just stopped to publish online utilities, it is a digital haven of people to take free powerful solutions from their browses in easy and fast ways.
