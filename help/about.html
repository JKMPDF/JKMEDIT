How it works

Most online tools send your data to their servers. This can be slow and less private. JKM Edit is built to do the work in your browser, and we don't keep copies of your files.

Who uses it

Students, freelancers, and small businesses use these tools for quick edits, file changes, and simple calculations. The site works well on computers, tablets, and phones.
