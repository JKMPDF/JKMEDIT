The "Zero-Server" Difference

Most online tools require you to upload your sensitive PDFs, private images, or proprietary code to a cloud server to be processed. This is slow and poses a massive security risk.

At JKM Edit, we leverage modern browser technologies (like WebAssembly and advanced JavaScript APIs) to do all the heavy lifting locally on your computer or smartphone. This means we literally do not have the capability to see, store, or share your data. It is a mathematically secure way to handle your workflow.
