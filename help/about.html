About JKM Edit

We build simple, free tools that help you get things done — without compromising your privacy.

Our Mission

JKM Edit was born out of a simple frustration: too many online tools require sign-ups, upload your files to unknown servers, or lock basic features behind paywalls. We set out to fix that.

Our goal is straightforward — give you a set of very useful tools that work instantly in your browser, keep your data on your device, and never ask for a credit card.

How It Works

Most online tools work by sending your files to a remote server, processing them there, and sending back the result. This is slow, uses bandwidth, and exposes your data to third parties.

JKM Edit takes a different approach. We built our tools using modern browser APIs — WebAssembly, Canvas, and client-side JavaScript — so the heavy lifting happens on your machine. Your files never leave your browser.

Who Uses JKM Edit

Students use it to merge lecture slides and compress assignments. Freelancers rely on it for quick image edits and invoice generation. Small business owners track daily transactions with our ledger tool. Developers use the code generators and formatters daily.

In short — anyone who needs to get something done with a file, quickly and without hassle, finds JKM Edit useful.
