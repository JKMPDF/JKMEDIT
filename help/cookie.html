Cookie Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, phone, or other device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide useful information about how visitors interact with them.

About your files: Because JKM Edit processes everything in your browser, we never use cookies or local storage to upload, save, or access your PDFs, images, or code. Your files remain on your device at all times.

2. How We Use Cookies

JKM Edit uses cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage) for the following purposes:

  • Remembering your preferences (such as whether you've dismissed the cookie banner).
  • Understanding how visitors use our tools so we can improve them.
  • Displaying relevant advertisements to keep our tools free.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize the cookies used on our site as follows:

A. Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the website to function. For example, we use browser storage to remember that you've accepted the cookie banner so you aren't prompted repeatedly. You can block these cookies, but certain parts of the site may not work correctly.

B. Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us measure traffic and understand where our visitors come from, allowing us to improve the site. They show us which tools are most popular and how people navigate the site. All data collected is anonymous. We use Google Analytics for this purpose.

C. Advertising Cookies
To keep JKM Edit free, we display advertisements. We use Google AdSense, which employs cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our website or other sites you've browsed.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We also utilize third-party cookies for analytics and advertising. These include:

  • Google Analytics: For tracking website performance. (Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements.

5. Your Choices: Managing & Disabling Cookies

Visitors from certain regions may see a cookie consent banner that allows them to manage their preferences.

You have the option to accept or reject cookies. You can also modify your browser settings to accept or refuse cookies. Refer to your browser's help documentation for specific instructions.

To opt out of personalized ads served by Google AdSense, visit Google's Ad Settings. You can also visit www.aboutads.info to opt out of third-party advertising cookies.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for other operational or legal reasons. Please check this page regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies.
