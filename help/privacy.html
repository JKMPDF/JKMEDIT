Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to JKM Edit (at https://jkmedit.in/). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website and use our tools.

Browser-based processing: Our tools work directly in your browser whenever possible. Your files are not stored on our servers during normal use.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Because our tools work in your browser, we have no access to the content you process. Specifically, we never collect, view, or store:

  • PDF documents you merge, split, or compress.
  • Images you resize, convert, or crop.
  • Source code, JSON data, or Base64 strings you format or encode.
  • Passwords generated by our tools.

3. Data We Do Collect

While your files stay private, we do collect some information to help maintain and improve our website:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous information about how you use our website, including which pages you visit, how long you stay, and what type of device you use.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to make the website work (like remembering if you closed the cookie banner) and analytical cookies to understand how people use our site.

4. Third-Party Services and Advertising

To keep JKM Edit free, we use some third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to see how many people visit our website. This tool may use cookies to collect anonymous information about your visit.
  • Google AdSense: We show ads from Google. Google uses cookies to show ads based on your visits to our website or other sites. You can choose not to see personalized ads by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Data Security

We use strong security measures to protect our website from attacks. JKM Edit uses HTTPS encryption to keep your connection to our website secure. Since your files are processed in your browser, the security of your documents mainly depends on how secure your own device and network are.

6. Links to Other Websites

Our website may have links to other websites or services that are not owned or controlled by JKM Edit. We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of those other sites.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have specific rights about your personal data. Since we don't store user accounts or personal files, there's almost no personal data for us to keep or delete. If you're concerned about analytics data or cookies, you can clear your browser cookies anytime or turn them off in your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy sometimes. We will let you know about changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. We recommend checking this Privacy Policy occasionally for any changes.
