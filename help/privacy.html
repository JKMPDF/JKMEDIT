Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to JKM Edit (at https://jkmedit.in/). Your privacy matters to us, and we are committed to being transparent about how we handle your information when you visit our website and use our tools.

Browser-based processing: Our tools run directly in your browser whenever possible. Your files are never stored on our servers during normal use.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Because our tools process files locally in your browser, we have no access to the content you work with. Specifically, we never collect, view, or store:

  • PDF documents you merge, split, or compress.
  • Images you resize, convert, or crop.
  • Source code, JSON data, or Base64 strings you format or encode.
  • Passwords generated by our tools.

3. Data We Do Collect

While your files remain private, we do collect some basic information to keep the website running smoothly and to understand how people use it:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous information about how you interact with our website — which pages you visit, how long you stay, and what type of device and browser you're using.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to make the website function properly (like remembering that you dismissed the cookie banner) and analytical cookies to understand traffic patterns.

4. Third-Party Services and Advertising

To keep JKM Edit free for everyone, we partner with a few third-party services. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand visitor numbers and behavior. This tool may use cookies to collect anonymous information about your visit.
  • Google AdSense: We display ads through Google. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect our website from attacks. JKM Edit uses HTTPS encryption to secure the connection between your browser and our servers. Since your files are processed locally on your device, the security of your documents largely depends on the security of your own device and network.

6. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites or services that are not owned or controlled by JKM Edit. We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data. Since we don't maintain user accounts or store personal files, there is very little personal data for us to retain or delete. If you have concerns about analytics data or cookies, you can clear your browser cookies at any time or disable them entirely through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
