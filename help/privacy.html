Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to JKM Edit (accessible at https://jkmedit.in/). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we process, collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our utility tools.

Client-Side Processing: Our tools process files directly within your browser whenever technically possible. Files are not stored on our servers during standard processing operations.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Because of our client-side architecture, we have no access to the content you process on our website. Specifically, we never collect, view, or store:

  • PDF documents you merge, split, or compress.
  • Images you resize, convert, or crop.
  • Source code, JSON data, or Base64 strings you format or encode.
  • Passwords generated by our tools.

3. Data We Do Collect

While your files remain completely private, we do collect certain non-personally identifiable information to maintain and improve our website:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics regarding how you navigate our website, including pages visited, time spent on pages, browser type, and device type.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to keep the website functional (such as remembering if you dismissed the cookie banner) and analytical cookies to understand traffic patterns.

4. Third-Party Services and Advertising

To keep JKM Edit 100% free, we use third-party services that may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to measure website traffic. This tool may use cookies to gather anonymous data about your visit.
  • Google AdSense: We display advertisements served by Google. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect the site from malicious attacks. JKM Edit uses HTTPS encryption to ensure that the connection between your browser and our website is secure. However, because your files are processed locally, the security of your documents relies primarily on the security of your own device and network.

6. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by JKM Edit. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

7. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data. Because we do not store user accounts or personal files, there is virtually no personal data for us to retain or delete. However, if you have concerns about analytics data or cookies, you can clear your browser cookies at any time or disable them completely via your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
