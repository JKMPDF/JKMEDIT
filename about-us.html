About JKM Edit

Welcome to JKM Edit, a platform that has been created out of passion, creativity and the need to make digital tools fun, simple and useful for everyone.

Our Story

We started JKM Edit in 2025 from the thought of creating a platform to give people useful tools, digital education and solutions, all without any complicated product or software or paywalled subscription packs.

The platform has been founded by Mahendra Purohit who started his journey in website making, editing tools and utility sites. And as he kept on having more and more passion for technology and digital tools, he turned the JKM Edit into a bigger platform for people now looking for easy to use websites and solutions online.

In the beginning, we were a site of simple utility pages and content experiments. But then we started adding more and more categories of tools and resources to JKM Edit, including PDF tools, image tools, finance tools, file converters, productivity resources, blogs and even educative content. All these updates and modifications have been done with one thing in mind: real life value for our users' success.

As we grew as a community the vision for JKM Edit expanded. Instead of just publishing tools we are turning the site into a complete digital resource station to provide free and efficient solutions at the click of a button at any time via the homepage.

Our Mission

So we've set ourselves a simple mission:

  • Build user friendly and dependable online tools
  • Make digital work easier and faster
  • Publish useful and high quality content
  • Continuously adapt in order to improve user experience and build into an irreplaceable online platform

We believe technology is one of the best tools. Technology is supposed to help people be more productive, save time and make their everyday task significantly easier. That's why we're constantly working on developing our lightweight, handy, and useful web-based tools for all devices.
