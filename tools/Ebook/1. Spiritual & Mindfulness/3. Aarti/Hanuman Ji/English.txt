Aarti Kije Hanuman Lala Ki, Dusht dalan Raghunath kala ki.

Jake bal se girivar kanpe, Rog dosh jake nikat na jhanke.

Anjani putra maha baldaayi, Santan ke prabhu sada sahayi.

De bira Raghunath pathaye, Lanka jari Sita sudhi laye.

Lanka so kot samudra-si khayi, Jat pavansut bar na layi.

Lanka jari asur sanhare, Siyaramji ke kaj savare.

Lakshman moorachit pade sakare, Anjani sanjivan praan ubare.

Paithi patal tori jam-kare, Ahiravan ki bhuja ukhare.

Bayein bhuja asur dal mare, Dahine bhuja santjan tare.

Sur-nar-muni jan aarti utare, Jai jai jai Hanuman uchare.

Kanchan thaar kapoor lau chhayi, Aarti karat Anjani maiya.

Jo Hanuman ji ki aarti gavai, Basi Baikunth param pad pavai.

Shri Hanuman Aarti ke bol samapan
