The Science of Compression: Quality vs. Size

One of the most common myths about PDF compression is that it totally destroys the PDF – crisp text becomes a cloudy mess, a bright photo becomes a blurry haze and huge PDF files become unreadable. Many try not to compress PDFs at all because of this. That's not true. JKM Edit's Compress PDF feature goes several steps beyond simple brute force reduction. It performs a detailed internal analysis of the structure of the PDF via intelligent algorithms.

Next, it finds the unnecessary chunks that actually use the most space in the PDF – often complex, high resolution images and redundant fonts – and optimizes the content without affecting perceptible, human quality. The new PDF will have all images downsampled to a resolution that is high enough to appear sharp on your screen but uses a dramatically smaller amount of data. Unnecessary metadata, hidden layers and redundant PDF objects will be eliminated. The PDF will be "smartly" compressed – the size reduction is significant (usually from 80% to 50% of the original) while visual quality will be hardly detectable from a reading standpoint. Whether you are compressing a text-based legal contract or a bright and colorful flyer, JKM Edit will produce a professional-looking PDF.
