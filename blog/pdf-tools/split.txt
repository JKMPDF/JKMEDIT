## How PDF splitting actually works (simple explanation)

When you upload a PDF into a split tool, the system:

1. reads all pages of the document
2. separates them into individual units
3. applies user-defined selection (pages or ranges)
4. generates new independent PDF files

This process does not change content — it only reorganizes structure.
