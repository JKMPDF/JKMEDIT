What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device whenever you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and info such as your login credentials. Cookies enhance functionality and user experience by gathering anonymous data.

Cookies do not normally carry personally identifying information, but some small info stored may be connected back to your preferences and browsing behaviour.
